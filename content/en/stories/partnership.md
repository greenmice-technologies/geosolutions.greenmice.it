## Problem

Infrastructure portfolios span large geographies and many asset classes. Monitoring and planning depend on reconciling spatial truth across surveys, operational systems, and analytics—often with long-lived datasets and strict audit expectations.

## Solution

We develop spatial systems that support **infrastructure monitoring and planning** with traceable workflows: governed datasets, repeatable pipelines, and services aligned to how owners and operators actually decide.

## Architecture

- Spatial pipelines with validation, reconciliation, and lineage suitable for corrections over time
- APIs that serve both interactive planning views and batch analytics
- Governance patterns that make datasets discoverable and auditable across teams

## Impact

More confident operational decisions; fewer mismatches between field reality, engineering models, and executive reporting.
