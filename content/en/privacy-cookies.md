## Privacy notice

This page explains how **Greenmice Geosolutions S.r.l.** handles personal data for visitors of this website.

If you contact us by email or contact forms, we process the data you provide (for example name, company, email, and message) only to respond to your request and manage the relationship.

## Cookies

This website uses technical cookies that are necessary for core functionality (for example language, theme, and session-level UX preferences).

At the moment, no non-essential profiling or marketing cookies are intentionally activated by default on this website.

If analytics or third-party non-essential cookies are enabled in future, this page and consent controls will be updated accordingly.

## Data minimization and retention

We only process data that is relevant to the request and retain it for as long as reasonably necessary for communication, legal, and operational purposes.
